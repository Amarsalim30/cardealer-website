# SEO Plan

## Focus
- Mobile-first local SEO for Nairobi and Kenya-based dealership searches
- Indexable vehicle detail pages and category pages
- Strong metadata and structured data for inventory

## URL Patterns
- `/inventory`
- `/inventory/new`
- `/inventory/used`
- `/inventory/imported`
- `/inventory/traded-in`
- `/cars/[slug]`
- `/financing`
- `/trade-in`
- `/about`
- `/contact`

## Title Patterns
- Home: `Cars for Sale in Nairobi | Summit Drive Motors`
- Category: `{Category} Cars for Sale in Nairobi | Summit Drive Motors`
- Vehicle: `{Year} {Make} {Model} for Sale in {Location} | Summit Drive Motors`

## Meta Description Guidance
- Keep descriptions under 160 characters.
- Mention stock type, location, and a direct value proposition.
- Include at least one clear lead-oriented verb such as call, view, or enquire.

## Target Keyword Themes
- cars for sale in Nairobi
- used cars in Kenya
- imported cars in Nairobi
- traded-in cars in Nairobi
- vehicle financing Kenya
- trade in your car Kenya

## Structured Data Targets
- `AutoDealer` site-wide
- `BreadcrumbList` for internal page hierarchy
- `ItemList` for inventory pages
- `Product` and `Offer` on vehicle detail pages

## Image Alt Rules
- Use year, make, model, color, and view angle when possible.
- Avoid generic alt text like "car image".
- Hero images should describe the visible vehicle clearly.

## Technical SEO Checklist
- Dynamic metadata for every vehicle page
- Sitemap generated from published vehicles only
- Robots file present
- Canonical URLs for key pages
- Internal links between homepage, categories, and similar vehicles
