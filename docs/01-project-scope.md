# Project Scope

## Goal
Build a production-ready dealership website MVP that helps a Nairobi-based dealer market inventory, build trust, and convert visits into enquiries.

## Business Outcomes
- Generate quote requests from inventory and vehicle detail pages.
- Increase WhatsApp and phone enquiries from high-intent buyers.
- Support test drive or viewing bookings with low-friction forms.
- Provide a simple back-office flow for keeping inventory accurate.

## Target Market
- Buyers in Nairobi and nearby markets looking for used, imported, and traded-in vehicles.
- Price-sensitive shoppers who want clear specs, availability, and finance guidance.
- Mobile-first users comparing dealers before contacting sales staff.

## Phase 1 Includes
- Home page with search, trust positioning, featured categories, and lead-focused CTAs.
- Inventory listing with filters and category-specific pages.
- Vehicle detail pages with gallery, specs, trust stack, and high-visibility CTAs.
- Financing, trade-in, about, and contact pages.
- Lead capture for quote, contact, financing, test drive, and trade-in.
- Minimal admin workflow for vehicle CRUD, publishing, sold status, featured status, and lead review.
- SEO baseline, sitemap, robots, structured data, and deployable environment configuration.

## Phase 1 Excludes
- Full CRM or sales pipeline management.
- Payment gateway, checkout, or booking deposit collection.
- Multi-branch staff roles, approval workflows, or audit dashboards.
- Advanced marketplace feed syndication.
- Multilingual support.

## Success Criteria
- Public site feels polished, mobile-first, and fast.
- Inventory and vehicle detail flow works end to end with demo data and production-ready persistence hooks.
- Lead forms validate cleanly and can persist to Supabase when configured.
- Admin users can manage vehicles and review incoming enquiries with minimal friction.
